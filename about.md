**Bananews** is a curated digest of tech news worth sharing — short, sharp, and sourced.

Each edition brings together a handful of notable stories from across the technology world. No filler, no hot takes — just the signals with direct links to the original sources so you can dive deeper.

Built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/).

### Why Bananews?

Because the tech news firehose is exhausting. Bananews is your friendly monkey handing you the ripest bananas — only the stories that actually matter, formatted for quick scanning and immediate access to the source.
